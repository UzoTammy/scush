Personal Income Tax (PIT) — Key Notes

Source: FIRS — governed by the Personal Income Tax Act Cap P8 LFN 2004 (as amended).

  • Imposed on income of individuals, corporate soles, communities, families, and trustees.
  • Consolidated Relief Allowance: ₦200,000 or 1% of gross income (whichever is higher), plus 20% of gross income.
  • Rates range from 7% to 24% depending on chargeable income.
  • Minimum tax of 1% of gross income applies where annual income is below ₦300,000.
  • Administered by FCT/State IRS for residents; FIRS for non-residents, Armed Forces, Police, and Foreign Service officers.
  • Annual return filing deadline: 31st March each year.
  • PAYE remittance deadline: 10th day of every succeeding month.
  • Employer must file emolument returns not later than 31st January annually.
  • Failure to file: fine of ₦5,000 plus ₦100/day or 6 months imprisonment.
  • Employer failure to file: ₦500,000 (corporate) or ₦50,000 (individual).
