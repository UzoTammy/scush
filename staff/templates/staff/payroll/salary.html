The Payroll Process

This module manages payroll for the Human Resource Department (HRD). Payroll has two components:

Salaries — processed monthly, includes:
  • Tax (PIT)
  • Credits, Incentives & Bonus
  • Base Salary
  • Debits, Shortages & Repayments
Gratuity — a reserve earned from performance, kept separately, processed under the payroll system pending resignation or retirement.
Steps for a Successful Payroll Run
  1. Ensure all staff profiles are updated
  2. Add Debit and Credit entries accordingly
  3. Update tax records
  4. Generate payroll
  5. Use Modify if outstanding amounts need to move to Net Pay (requires approval)
  6. Use Regenerate if any inputs change after generation (requires approval)

Recommended: generate payroll between the 25th and the last day of the month. Each payroll is categorised by period (one month).
