Company Policies

Company Policies are created to establish expectations and to provide guidance on how to consistently handle workplace situations. Although these policies cannot be all-encompassing, they provide direction regarding what is appropriate as well as inappropriate or unacceptable behaviour. This document help maintain order within the organization and ensure that staff are treated fairly and equally.

Our aim is to set up easily accessible company policies that is clear and help set behavioral and performance standard within the workplace. This document will help all staff avoid failure and its consequences. It will outline rules to abide by and therefore keep management accountable. No matter what, this document is not perfect and will always be subject to reviews from time to time with the intention to make it better as we share experiences together.

New Staff Policy

Every new staff must be able to read with understanding. They must be given access to this document and confirm that they have read, understood and accepted to abide by the rules of the document. This must be their first assignment as a staff of this company. They will also attest that all information provided during application are correct and if any information given is found to be incorrect or any necessary information not given will result to immediate sack.

Punctuality Policy
  • Late to Work Policy: Resumption time is 7:45am and closing time is 5:45pm. You are advised to resume before time and depart after closing time. However, If you must be late, send a written message (not excluding SMS or Chat) to your HRM giving your reason. This is subject to verification and approval before the permission can apply. Also, permission can be obtained earlier through application to be late. In this case, approval notice document MUST be obtained from HRM.

Note that permission given are in man-hours and will affect your punctuality score
