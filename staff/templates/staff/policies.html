Company Policies

Company Policies are created to establish expectations and to provide guidance on how to consistently handle workplace situations. Although these policies cannot be all-encompassing, they provide direction regarding what is appropriate as well as inappropriate or unacceptable behaviour. This document help maintain order within the organization and ensure that staff are treated fairly and equally.

Our aim is to set up easily accessible company policies that is clear and help set behavioral and performance standard within the workplace. This document will help all staff avoid failure and its consequences. It will outline rules to abide by and therefore keep management accountable. No matter what, this document is not perfect and will always be subject to reviews from time to time with the intention to make it better as we share experiences together.

Employment Policy

Any one who intend to work with Ozone, must tender an application letter, fill application form, provide valid and acceptable guarantor. With the application and the forms filled, he/she will be granted interview and be considered for employment or rejection. Should we discover wrong information or intention to deceive management, your employment will be terminated immediately no matter the level and the benefit you must have attained.

Congratulations!!! Now, you are part of us and must have received your employment letter, whatever is stated in the employment letter is an extension of our employment policy. First you must read the remainder of our policies, our procedures and our rules of engagement and any other necessary document that will help you succeed.

Punctuality Policy
  • Late to Work Policy: Resumption time is 7:45am and closing time is 5:45pm. You are advised to resume before time and depart after closing time. However, If you must be late, send a written message (not excluding SMS or chat) to HRM giving your reason. If approved permission can then be granted in man-hours. Better still, permission can be obtained earlier through an application. In this case, approval notice document MUST be obtained from HRD.
  • Absent from Work Policy: Should there be need to be absent from work, you will have to apply to HRM through your line head who is to recommend to the HRM whether to approve or not. The HRM will receive this application seven(7) days before the commencement date and will have to approve it three(3) days to the commencement date. However, only on an emergency case can we waive the durations. In both situations, approval notice document must be obtained else the request is null and void. A staff must take over the role pending your return and handover of the role back to you.

Note that permission given are in man-hours and will affect your punctuality score

Probation Period Policy

Every staff is given ninety(90) days probation period to prove him or herself. Within this period the company reserves the right to terminate your employment and also you have the right to terminate your employment. A one week notice by either party is required and doing so, company will pay you your accrued pay but if there is no prior notice or less than a week notice, you will loose your accrued salary. Also, within this period, you are not entitled to any bonus or incentive payable by the company, except on express approval by management.

Reassignment Policy

This involves moving you from one position and/or branch to another this occurs from time to time as necessary to ensure smooth operation of the company. Be reminded that our employment policy do not specifically pin you into a particular position or branch, therefore, we expect you to be ready to take up any reassignment designated to you at any time.

Resignation Policy

When you decide to resign your appointment, You must tender your resignation letter at least fourteen (14) days from your last date at work. During this period, you are expected to return all company properties in your custody and do carry out your designated tasks as usual. You must obtain clearance from your line head and every other necessary parties involved with you in carrying out your duties as a staff of this company. Quitting your job without communicating us in written

Termination Policy

This company reserves the right to terminate your appointment at any time deemed necessary the company will state the time of your last day at work. If termination is within the month and it is not related to fraud and gross misconduct, you will be paid in full your current basic salary at the end of the month and after due confirmation and clearance, any reserved bonuses accrued to you will be paid much later when company is certain that there are no outstanding liabilities in your name. However, you will incur immediate sack with no time to spare and no benefits whatsoever.
