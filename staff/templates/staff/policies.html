Company Policies

Our aim is to set up easily accessible company policies that is clear and help set behavioral and performance standard for our workplace. Company Policies are guidelines that help with the health, safety and accountability of employees, as well as their relationship with customers and colleagues. This document will help all staff avoid failure and its consequences. It will outline rules to abide by and therefore keep management accountable. No matter what, this document is not perfect and will always be subject to reviews from time to time with the intention to make it better as we share experiences together. This document will be categorized so that you can access it in parts.

New Staff Policy

Every new staff must be able to read with understanding. They must be given access to this document and confirm that they have read, understood and accepted to abide by the rules of the document. This must be their first assignment as a staff of this company. They will also attest that all information provided during application are correct and if any information given is found to be incorrect or any necessary information not given will result to immediate sack.
