Company Profile
Ozone F & L Trading Co. Ltd

Established in November 2009, Ozone F & L stands as a distinguished distribution company specializing in the one-way pack brands of global beverage giants, including Guinness Nigeria Plc, Nigeria Breweries Plc, and International Breweries Plc.

Market Dominance

As key players in the global beverage market, our partners have achieved unmatched control and recognition. Ozone has fortified its position as a trusted distribution ally.

Vision and Values

To become the most reliable and relevant one-way pack distribution company in Lagos. Core values: transparency, support, commitment, and reliability.

Achievements
  • Guinness 2015 Distributors Award: Highest Volume on MSS (National)
  • Nigerian Breweries 2020 Distributors Award: Funding Performance Award
