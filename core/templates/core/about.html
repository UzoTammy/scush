Our Mission & Values

To remain Lagos’s most trusted distributor of one-way packaging by fostering transparent partnerships and delivering unparalleled reliability to every business we serve.

Our Story

Founded in 2009 with a vision to revolutionize the accessibility of premium Nigerian beverages, Ozone has evolved into a strategic cornerstone for the nation’s brewing industry. Our pioneering focus on one-way packaging fostered landmark partnerships with Nigerian Breweries, Guinness Nigeria, and International Breweries—collectively representing over 95% of the market. By driving the initial demand that sparked industry-wide scaling, we have converted passion into a multi-billion naira enterprise. Today, we are leveraging this legacy of trust to expand our footprint beyond Lagos, utilizing both digital innovation and physical expansion to serve the entire nation and beyond.
