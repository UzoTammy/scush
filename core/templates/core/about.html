Welcome to Ozone F & L

Our Mission & Values:

At Ozone, we have worked hard to become the most reliable and relevant one-way pack distribution company in Lagos. Going forward, we remain committed to maintaining this status by continuing to prioritize transparency and support for our business partners. Our goal is to build on our reputation and further enhance our position as the trusted partner of choice for businesses in need to partner with a distributor in Lagos.

Our Story

In 2009, We set out on a mission to provide to Nigerians the more convinient made-in-Nigeria canned beer and malt products, this resulted to a unique partnership with first Nigerian Breweries and later with Guinness Nigeria and finally International Breweries. These three gaints control more than 95% of the market and our role in the one-way pack is so significant to them that we share a special relationship which we have extended to our customers. Our initial effort and passion to distribute beer and malt in this format resulted to all three investing heavily in scale-up. The volume we push to the market today has resulted in billions of naira turnover and has made us a house hold name. Leveraging on the good name, we seek to pursue our ultimate goal of spreading to other parts of the country through our online platform and physical expansion beyond Lagos. Our trust in our relationship with you, gives us the confidence to keep moving.
