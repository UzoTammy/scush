Ozone F & L Trading Company Limited is a registered company in Nigeria, currently operating in Lagos state. Founded in 2009 by Uzo Tammy Nwokoro, it has been operating as a distribution company with the major brewing companies in Nigeria. We also distribute for a hygiene company Hayat Kimya.

Our partnership with

  • Nigerian Brewery(subsidiary of Heineken)
  • Guinness Nigeria Plc(subsidiary of Diageo)
  • International Brewery(subsidiary of AB-Inbev)
  • Hayat Kimya Nigeria Plc(Subsidiary of Hayat Kimya International
forms the trade arm of our business while other investments in banks and start-up companies compliment our business.

Our staff strength hovers between 35 and 40, with customer base of about 1500. We are committed to developing whole sale businesses in Lagos and beyond. We operate from six of our branches located in

  • Vino, Genesis and Front Gate all in Trade Fair
  • Lagos, Lagos Island
  • Stardom, Iju
  • Badagry, Badagry Town

Operating in a harsh and unpredictable environment, we still seek to transform into a cooperate, online company with the hope of trading evenly across Nigeria and beyond. With passionate and committed staff we are able to get along. Meet some of the staff here.
