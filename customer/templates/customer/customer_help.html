Customer Help Return
Adding a Customer

Go to Customer Home and click Add Customer. Fill in the form and click Create.

Required fields: Business Name, Address, Mobile, Cluster, Region, and Classification. Contact Person and Email are optional.

Finding a Customer

Click All Customers from the home page to open the customer list. From there you can:

  • Use the search bar to find by name, mobile, or any visible column.
  • Use the filter dropdown (top-left of the table) to narrow by status or region — Active, Inactive, Lagos, Diaspora, or Outside Lagos.
  • Click a cluster tile on the home page to see all customers in that delivery cluster.
Viewing a Customer Profile

In the customer list, click the business name (shown as a link) to open the full profile. The profile shows contact details, classification, credit status, and activity state.

Updating a Customer

Open the customer's profile, then click Update in the top navigation. Edit the relevant fields and click Update to save.

Activating or Disabling a Customer

Customer profiles are never deleted — they are either Active or Inactive.

To change a customer's status, open their profile and use the Update button to toggle the Active checkbox. Inactive customers are excluded from active-only list filters.

Credit Records

Credit records are managed separately from profiles. From the home page, click Credit Records.

  • To create a credit record for a customer, open their profile — a Create Credit Record link appears if none exists yet.
  • To update a credit limit or mark a customer as blacklisted, open the credit list and use the actions from there.
  • A customer's current credit balance and limit are shown on their profile page.
