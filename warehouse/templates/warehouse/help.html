Navigating the Facilities Home Page

There sections of the facilities management page are
  • Facilities
  • Rent
  • Levy

Add Record to Facility

  • Click the Facility
  • Below the table (if there are records) is an Add button click to add new facility
  • Click Submit to Save

Update Facility

  • Click the Facilities link
  • Click a facility on the name column to display it's page
  • Click on Update, an update form will display
  • Adjust record and click Submit to Save

Pay Rent

  • Click on "Rent is Due" on the Rented Facilities and Due dates table
  • A pop-up meeasge will apear for you to confirm, click Yes
  • A Pay Rent form will appear, fill accordingly
  • Click Submit to Save

Update or Adjust Rent Paid

  • Click on the rent link, the list of paid rent will appear
  • Click on the Paid rent of interest, the form will display
  • Adjust as desired and Click Submit to Save

Pay Levy from "Rent is Due"

  • Click on "Rent is Due" on the Rented Facilities and Due dates table
  • A pop-up meeasge will apear for you to confirm, click Yes
  • A Pay Rent form will appear, fill accordingly
  • Click Submit to Save

Pay Levy

  • Click the Levy Link on the Facility Management Home page
  • Below the list of paid levy displayed, Click on Pay Levy
  • Pay Levy form will display, fill up accordingly
  • Click Submit to Save

Update Paid Levy

  • Click on the rent link, the list of paid rent will appear
  • Click on the Paid rent of interest, the form will display
  • Adjust as desired and Click Submit to Save
