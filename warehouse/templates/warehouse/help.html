Introduction

Our business is not run from one location but multiple locations and with many stores. They are of different sizes and types. Some are small lock-up shops and yet some are of warehouse size, we also use them as either sellout points or for storage (warehousing). These properties are retained because of the rents we pay on each of them except for some that we own.

For proper accountability and effective management of these properties, we have decided to integrate them into the SCusH app for administrative

Our aim is to

  • Keep inventory of these properties
  • Be informed when rent is due
  • To differentiate what we own and what we pay rent on
  • To store the value of the properties we own
  • To be able to evaluate monthly and annually what it cost the company to maintain these properties

To update a store, from the Stores Home Page and by the sidebar,

  • Click All and you will see stores table with name column
  • Click the name link of the particular store of interest
  • By the sidebar, click Update
  • Make the adequate changes on the populated form and click submit to apply the changes or return to cancel
Note: The submit button is at the bottom of the page
